THE PROBLEM

Why U-Tracksis exists

Thesis advisers juggle lecture loads, laboratory classes, and research — all while supervising multiple groups.

Feedback gets delayed. Revisions get lost in inboxes.

This site replaces manual communication and disorganized files with one transparent system for monitoring, scheduling, and documenting progress.

All in one place. Accessible to your panel anytime.

HOW IT WORKS

Four simple steps to a smoother thesis

01

Coordinators set milestones

Define the term's deliverables and deadlines once. Apply to every group

02

Advisers are assigned

Admin matches advisers to group. Each advisers sees only their cohort

03

Students submit & iterate

Groups upload drafts, receive revisions, and update their progress bar.

04

Theses get approved

Finals approved are logged, archived and surfaced in analytics.

PROJECT FEATURES

Everything your thesis needs

Milestone tracking

Visual progress bars and cohort-wide dashboards keep every group on schedule.

Document revisions

Versioned uploads with adviser remarks, approve/reject status, and full history.

Consultation scheduling

Request, confirm, and log every consultation with timestamped records.

Structured feedback

Comments tied to milestones and documents never lost in an email thread.

Deadline notifications
