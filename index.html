THE PROBLEM

Thesis advisers juggle lecture loads, laboratory classes, and research - all while supervising multiple groups. Feedback gets delayed. Revisions get lost in inboxes.

This site replaces manual communication and disorganized files with one transparent system for monitoring, scheduling, and documenting progress.
